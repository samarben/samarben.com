About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

The Andalucia Beach Hotel Residence is a hotel classified in the luxury 4 **** category, and presents a future investment for the Bizerte region. Located in the Sidi Salem Tourist Zone, the hotel is 1 km from the city center and the Bizerte bridge, and 63 km from the capital Tunis. The construction works were started in 2010 and it was launched on Jun 26, 2012, its Managing Director is Mr.Akram Cherif.
